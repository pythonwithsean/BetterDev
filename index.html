LeetCode Strategy

  • 1
    Start with Easy problems. Build confidence and pattern recognition before attempting Medium.
  • 2
    Learn patterns, not solutions. Understand why a technique works. Patterns transfer; memorized solutions don't.
  • 3
    Do the Blind 75 / NeetCode 150. Complete them in topic order rather than randomly.
  • 4
    Time yourself. 20 min Easy, 30 min Medium, 45 min Hard. If stuck, look at the solution then redo it the next day.
  • 5
    Brute force first, then optimize. A working O(n^2) beats a broken O(n). Start simple, find the bottleneck, improve.
